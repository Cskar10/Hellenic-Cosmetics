ABOUT US

Born from the blend of Greek heritage and modern Australian artistry, Hellenic Cosmetics was created with one vision — to make every client feel like the best version of themselves. Inspired by the grace and strength of the goddesses, every service is designed as more than a beauty treatment; it’s a moment of care, confidence, and connection. After moving to Australia in 2012, I found a new home that allowed my passion for beauty to grow and my dream to take shape. Years of learning, evolving, and perfecting my craft led me here — to create a space where self-care feels empowering and divine.

At Hellenic Cosmetics, beauty is not about changing who you are, but celebrating it.

Future Bookings Policy

To ensure a smooth and professional experience for all clients, please review the following booking terms:

Deposits & Booking
A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. Bookings are confirmed only once the deposit has been received. Prices and availability are subject to change without notice.

Rescheduling
Appointments may be rescheduled once with a minimum of 48 hours’ notice. Reschedules made after this period will result in the forfeiture of the deposit.

Cancellations & No-Shows
Cancellations within 48 hours or missed appointments will forfeit the deposit. Repeated no-shows or late cancellations may require full prepayment for future bookings.

Late Arrivals
Please arrive on time. Clients arriving more than 10 minutes late may have their appointment shortened or rescheduled at the artist’s discretion.

Future Appointments
Frequent last-minute changes may affect future booking availability. Clients are encouraged to book in advance to secure preferred dates and times.
